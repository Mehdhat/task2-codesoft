Frequently Asked Questions

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and act like humans.

What is Cloud Computing?

Cloud computing is the delivery of computing services—including servers, storage, databases, networking, software, and more—over the internet ("the cloud").

How Does Artificial Intelligence Work?

AI works by combining large amounts of data with fast, iterative processing and intelligent algorithms, allowing the software to learn automatically from patterns or features in the data.

What Are the Benefits of Cloud Computing?

Some of the key benefits of cloud computing include cost savings, scalability, flexibility, efficiency, and accessibility.
